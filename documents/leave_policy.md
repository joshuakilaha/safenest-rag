# SafeNest Leave Policy

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to define the types of leave available to employees of SafeNest and to ensure fair and consistent application of leave entitlements.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of SafeNest.

## 3. Types of Leave

### 3.1 Annual Leave
- Employees are entitled to 21 working days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave days accrue monthly.
- Unused leave may be carried forward for a maximum of 6 months into the next year.

### 3.2 Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to up to 14 days of paid sick leave annually.
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.

### 3.3 Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to 90 calendar days of paid maternity leave.
- Maternity leave may be extended with unpaid leave upon request.

### 3.4 Paternity Leave
- Male employees are entitled to 14 calendar days of paid paternity leave.

### 3.5 Compassionate Leave
- Employees are entitled to 5 days of compassionate leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member.

## 4. Leave Application Process
- All leave requests must be submitted through the company HR system at least 7 days in advance.
- Emergency leave must be reported to the supervisor as soon as possible.

## 5. Approval
- Leave approval is subject to operational requirements and supervisor approval.

## 6. Public Holidays
- SafeNest observes all officially recognized public holidays.

## 7. Policy Review
This policy will be reviewed annually or as required.
