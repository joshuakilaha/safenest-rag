# SafeNest Remote Work Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the guidelines for employees working remotely to ensure productivity, security, and accountability.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees approved for remote work at SafeNest.

## 3. Eligibility
- Remote work is available to employees whose roles support off-site work.
- Approval must be obtained from the employee’s supervisor.

## 4. Working Hours
- Employees must adhere to standard working hours (8:00 AM – 5:00 PM).
- Availability during working hours is mandatory.

## 5. Communication
- Employees must remain reachable via email, phone, or company communication tools.
- Daily or weekly check-ins may be required.

## 6. Performance Expectations
- Employees are expected to maintain the same level of productivity as in-office work.
- Deliverables must be completed on time.

## 7. Equipment and Security
- Employees must use company-approved devices.
- Public Wi-Fi must not be used without a VPN.
- Confidential information must not be accessed in public spaces.

## 8. Data Protection
- All company data accessed remotely must follow the SafeNest Data Protection Policy.
- Devices must be password-protected.

## 9. Health and Safety
- Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe working environment at home.

## 10. Revocation of Remote Work
- Remote work privileges may be revoked if performance or compliance issues arise.

## 11. Policy Review
This policy will be reviewed periodically.
