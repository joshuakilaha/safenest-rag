# SafeNest Data Protection Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy outlines how SafeNest collects, processes, stores, and protects personal and company data in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who access SafeNest systems or data.

## 3. Data Classification
Data is classified into the following categories:
- Public Data
- Internal Data
- Confidential Data
- Sensitive Personal Data

## 4. Data Collection
- Only necessary data should be collected.
- Data must be collected lawfully and transparently.

## 5. Data Usage
- Data must only be used for its intended purpose.
- Unauthorized use of data is strictly prohibited.

## 6. Data Storage
- All sensitive data must be encrypted.
- Data must be stored in secure systems with access controls.

## 7. Access Control
- Access to data is granted based on job roles.
- Employees must not share login credentials.

## 8. Data Retention
- Data must be retained only for as long as necessary.
- Obsolete data must be securely deleted.

## 9. Data Breach Management
- All data breaches must be reported within 24 hours.
- The IT team will investigate and mitigate risks immediately.

## 10. Employee Responsibilities
- Employees must complete mandatory data protection training.
- Any suspicious activity must be reported immediately.

## 11. Compliance
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action.

## 12. Policy Review
This policy is reviewed annually.
